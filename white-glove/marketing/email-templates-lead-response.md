# Email Templates — Lead Response

---

## Qualified Lead Response

Use when someone reaches out with a real project.

```
Subject: Re: [Their Subject]

Hey [Name],

Thanks for reaching out.

Your project sounds interesting — [specific thing they mentioned].

A few quick questions before we schedule a call:

1. What's the current state of the project? (idea stage, building, live?)
2. What specifically are you trying to learn? (methodology, specific tool, architecture?)
3. What's your timeline?

If this sounds like a fit, I'll send over a Google Meet link for a 20-min discovery call.

Talk soon,
Jeremy

--
jeremy@intentsolutions.io
jeremylongshore.com
```

---

## Decline Template

Use when someone doesn't have a real project or isn't a fit.

```
Subject: Re: [Their Subject]

Hey [Name],

Thanks for reaching out.

Based on what you described, I don't think "Learn with Jeremy" is the right fit — it's designed for people who already have a defined project and want to learn the methodology hands-on.

A few resources that might help:
• claudecodeplugins.io — 280+ free plugins
• My GitHub: github.com/jeremylongshore

Good luck with [their project]!

Jeremy
```

Use after qualifying questions are answered.

```

Hey [Name],

Here's a Google Meet link: [INSERT LINK]

Pick a time that works:
• [Option 1]
• [Option 2]
• [Option 3]

Talk soon,
Jeremy
```

jeremy@intentsolutions.io
