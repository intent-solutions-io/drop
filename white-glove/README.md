## Service Tiers

### Tier 1: Self-Service ($0)
- Installation guide PDF
- Basic CLAUDE.md template
- Email support

### Tier 2: Guided Setup ($99)
- 30-min video call
- Screen-share installation
- Custom CLAUDE.md for their workflow
- 1 week email support

### Tier 3: Full White Glove ($299)
- 60-min discovery + setup call
- Remote installation (they share screen)
- Custom CLAUDE.md + 3 workflow templates
- First project walkthrough together
- 30-day email/chat support
- 1 follow-up call

### Tier 4: Team Onboarding ($999)
- Up to 5 team members
- Team CLAUDE.md with shared standards
- Slack channel support (30 days)
- 2 group training sessions

Before call:

After call:
- [ ] Send follow-up email with resources
