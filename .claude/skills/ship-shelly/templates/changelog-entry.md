## Tone Guidelines

When writing entries, remember:

1. **Conversational but professional** - Write like you're updating a colleague, not filing a report
2. **Show progress, not activity** - "Created X" not "Worked on X"
3. **Highlight decisions** - Shelly needs to validate choices before we continue
4. **Respect the deliberate pace** - Don't rush or overpromise
5. **Build trust through transparency** - If something went wrong, say so

## What NOT to Include

- Stream of consciousness / raw notes
- Every minor commit message
- Technical jargon without explanation
- Complaints or frustrations
- Promises about future work without Shelly's buy-in
